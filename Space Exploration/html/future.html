Upcoming Missions

Artemis I

Expected Launch: November 2024

NASA's Artemis program aims to return humans to the Moon, paving the way for future Mars exploration.

James Webb Space Telescope (JWST)

Launch Date: Launched in December 2021, but ongoing missions until 2030.

JWST will continue to transform our understanding of the universe, observing the first galaxies and distant exoplanets.

Mars Sample Return Mission

Expected Launch: 2028

This mission aims to return samples collected by the Perseverance rover back to Earth for detailed analysis.

Europa Clipper

Expected Launch: 2024

This mission will study Jupiter's moon Europa, which may harbor conditions suitable for life.

Artemis II

Expected Launch: 2026

The first crewed flight of the Artemis program, aiming to orbit the Moon and pave the way for lunar landings.
